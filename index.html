I AM THE BABY <3

For Ludum Dare 33.

Objectives: You are the baby. Cause as much mayhem as possible before your parents are back. Don't die.

Hint 1: Bump into furniture to destroy. Faster = More damage.

Hint 2: You can tame machines by rubbing them. Tamed machines can attack other enemies.

Hint 3: Wearing a pair of sunglesses makes you invincible.
